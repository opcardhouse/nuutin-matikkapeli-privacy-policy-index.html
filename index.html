Privacy Policy – Nuutin Matikkapeli

Last updated: 2026

Developer: O-P Card House

Contact: opcardhouse@opsahkopalvelut.fi

Introduction

O-P Card House respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the mobile game Nuutin Matikkapeli.

Nuutin Matikkapeli is a child-friendly educational math game.

Information We Collect

Nuutin Matikkapeli does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not collect:

  • name
  • email address
  • phone number
  • home address
  • precise location
  • photos, videos, or audio
  • contacts
  • payment information
  • advertising ID
  • personal identifiers

No Account Registration

The app does not require account registration or login.

Users can play the game without creating an account or providing any personal information.

Game Data

Game progress, settings, scores, or similar game-related data may be stored locally on the user's device.

This data is used only to make the game work on the device.

This data is not sent to O-P Card House or external servers.

Users can remove locally stored data by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

Nuutin Matikkapeli does not use third-party services that collect personal data.

The app does not use Firebase, analytics services, advertising services, or social media tracking tools.

Analytics and Advertising

Nuutin Matikkapeli does not use advertising.

Nuutin Matikkapeli does not use analytics.

Nuutin Matikkapeli does not sell or share user data with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

Nuutin Matikkapeli is designed to be suitable for children and families.

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

The app does not allow users to communicate with other users, share personal information, or upload content.

Data Sharing

O-P Card House does not sell, rent, or share personal data.

Because the app does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to share with third parties.

Security

Nuutin Matikkapeli is designed to work locally on the user's device without collecting personal information.

Data Retention and Deletion

O-P Card House does not retain personal data from Nuutin Matikkapeli.

Any local game data stored on the device can be deleted by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future.
